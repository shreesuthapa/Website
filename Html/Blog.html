Impact of Internet in our daily lives.

The internet domains many aspects of our daily lives. Internet has been addiction to many students as well as their parents. The growth in different technologies causes the psychological, social, educational, occupational problems. Internet are recommended by educational sector for researching about different topic, to gain extra knowledge as Internet is a vas resource that covers every sector. We can research about any topic by the help of Internet, as many users use the term Internet surfing. The social media influence in students is real, they are so much addicted in Instagrams, Facebook, Tiktok and games like Pubg, Freefire, Candy Crush, and many more which are all played online.

Internet addiction has been accompanied by major problems such as the drop in grades, reduce interest in educational field, anxiety, communication gap, and many small issues are created because of students being addicted to Internet or more likely social medias and online games. The excessive use of internet also has impact in physical and mental state of students. The research has proved that students doesn't seem interested in playing or roaming around outside the house, they enjoy sitting in a chair for hours and enjoy playing games, surfing in Internet, and in social medias. This creates a mental trauma and physical damage in different body parts. The physical exercise by roaming around and inhaling fresh air is not possible by sitting in room, this also creates lack of social life students are lacking communication skills and they are not being able to digest the real world problems. They enjoy chatting rather than speaking in person. These issues have been addressed by many parents and teachers. The internet has a huge impact on students lives. However, if they can manage then Internet has huge merits in our daily lives.

Being a student, moreover being a technological field students thinking practically I have acknowledge the use of Internet more that its demerits. The impact of Internet not only counts students wasting their time in social medias, playing video games, surfing YouTube but also take a look on the beautiful intelligence created by many technologist. Internet: During Coronavirus

Internet has been very beneficial during this pandemic situation. We are all aware about the crucial situation we all have been going throught. The whole nation is under lockdown and there is no any validation on when everything will be back to normal. During this pandemic situation everything has been dependent on Internet, the online education, online business and work from home is all possible because of Internet. Life would have been different if there was no Internet. Atleast, student are involved in online education than not being able to study. The relatives are in contact with each other via social medias that all comes under use of Internet. Therefore, Internet has been beneficial during this pandemic time.
